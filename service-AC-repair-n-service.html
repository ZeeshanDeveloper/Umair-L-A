You depend on your air conditioner to blow cool, refreshing air during hot summers. If your AC unit isn’t functioning the way it should, you need the expertise of an experienced and local HVAC company. U M Air Inc. is perfect choice for AC repairs.

From our location in Downey, we have served almost all parts of Los Angeles and Orange County for over 12 years. Let us use our experience to troubleshoot your AC system and help you enjoy cool, fresh air once more.

Why Isn’t Your AC Working?

If you notice any of the following, it is a good idea to call us right away to schedule a repair:

  • Uneven cooling throughout your home
  • Strange smells coming from unit
  • High humidity level
  • Failure to start
  • Sudden increase in energy costs
  • Unusual sounds coming from unit

How Can We Help?

U M Air Inc. has a highly trained team who know how to diagnose AC problems quickly and efficiently. We don’t need much time to figure out what’s wrong with your AC system. And once we determine the problem, we can provide fast and thorough AC repairs.
